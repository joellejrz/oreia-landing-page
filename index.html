The Architecture

Privacy by math,
not by promise.

Three layers of mathematical privacy guarantee your anonymity before any data leaves your device.

01

Generalization

Specific data is transformed into broad categories locally. Your exact age becomes "30–35." Your city becomes a country. Details dissolve while patterns remain.

02

Differential Privacy

A mathematical "coin flip" injects noise into your data. Your true traits are mixed with randomized decoys, granting plausible deniability while keeping aggregate statistics accurate.

03

K-Anonymity — The Rule of 5

No data point is ever examined if fewer than 5 people share that category. If your profile is too unique, the AI blurs it locally. You are never a data point of one.

What is Oreia The Journal?

Oreia is a private AI journal for dream work, shadow work, and self-reflection. It combines local AI, encrypted storage, and a zero-knowledge architecture so your writing stays yours.

Is Oreia a private AI journal?

Yes. Oreia is designed as a private AI journal from the ground up. Your entries are encrypted with AES-256, processed locally, and never uploaded as raw text to our servers.

Does Oreia work offline?

Yes. The core journaling and local AI experience are designed to run on your device, which means you can use Oreia offline while keeping your reflections private.

Can I use Oreia for dream journaling and shadow work?

Absolutely. Oreia was built for dream journaling, emotional pattern tracking, archetype reflection, and shadow work. The AI is designed to support deeper self-inquiry, not to replace therapy.
